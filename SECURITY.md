# Security policy

If you discover a security issue in the Fluidic node runtime, please report it responsibly.

## Contact

Email **hello@fed-labs.xyz** with the subject line `Fluidic security`.

## Responsible disclosure

- Do not exploit a vulnerability beyond what is needed to confirm it.
- Do not access, modify, or delete other users' data or accounts.
- Provide a clear description, reproduction steps, and the affected commit or version.
- Give us reasonable time to investigate and release a fix before disclosing publicly.
- We will acknowledge receipt of your report and keep you updated on our progress.

## Scope

In scope: the Fluidic mesh node source code in this repository.

Out of scope: social engineering, physical attacks, and third-party services not operated by the Fluidic project.
